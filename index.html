ABOUT ABOUT MERCURE RANDBURG “The Mercure Johannesburg Randburg Hotel near Sandton, South Africa’s financial and commercial hub, is an ideal location for business travellers, tourists and groups looking for value that does not compromise on quality services.”

– Hotel Manager

The Mercure Johannesburg Randburg Hotel near Sandton, South Africa’s financial and commercial hub, is a perfect place to enjoy sundowners in the bar or on the outdoor patio – and is an ideal location for your next trip. 104 stylish guest rooms come with free, unlimited Wi-Fi, air-conditioning and TVs. Our business hotel also has free parking and 2 meeting rooms that can accommodate up to 20 people for business events. Our hotel with breakfast also offers light lunches and dinner until 9:30pm.

Looking for a hotel near Lanseria Airport? Our hotel in Johannesburg is ideally located. You can also enjoy entertainment nearby at the Ferndale on Republic Shopping Centre, the Walter Sisulu Sports Hall, the Randburg Sports Centre or the Golf course. Any questions about what to do while you’re here? We can help! You could take a walk to the Ferndale on Republic shopping centre, next to our hotel, or discover the various local attractions like Soweto and the Cradle of Humankind or the Apartheid Museum.
